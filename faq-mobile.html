Q: Why Busgazer

A: Waiting for buses in painful, especially in extreme weather, e.g. the chilly winter in Pittsburgh. With Busgaer, you know exactly when you buses will arrives, so that you can plan accordingly.

Q: How Busgazer works?

A: Busgazer predicts bus arrival times based on realtime data source provided by the the Port Authority of Allegheny County (PAAC). The realtime predications are calculated from realtime GPS location data collected from tracking devices installed on buses.

Q: Who are we?

A: Busgazer is developed as a side project by a group of students at the Computer Science Department at PITT. We made it available to the public to give back to the community. Our frist iOS App was released on November 8, 2014, at the beginning of the winter. And we released our first Android App on December 4, 2014.

Currently it is running with out external funding. We have put in months of fulltime engineering efforts as well as money from our own pocket to build and improve Busgazer. If you like it and find it helpful, please share this App with your friends or like us on Facebook. You can also support us via in-App purchase. Your support is our forward power!

Q: What are coins used for?

A: 1) One coin is consumed each *active day* you use Busgazer. Coins are not consumed if you did not use our service.

2) Coins can be used to unlock premium features.

Q: How to gain more coins?

Currently, you can get more coins by either refering friends or donation via "Support Us".

Q: What about the referral program?

A: You get 100 coins for each successful referral. By sending the referral link to your friends, you will get 50 coins if they clicked the link, installed Busgazer, and signed up. And you get the remaining 50 coins, if they successfully verifed their email address.
